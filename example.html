An Example: Training of a perfect Pokémon

General Training Checklist

To get started competing in online battles, it is not sufficient just to catch the Pokémon you want to use. There are some mandatory steps to carry out in order to make your Pokémon viable for online fighting. Here you can see what you have to do:
  • Reach level 100: Use "Rare Candy" or "EXP Candy"
  • Choose recommended nature:Use "[chosen nature] Mint"
  • Maximize DV: Use "Gold Bottle Cap" for maximizing ALL DVs or use "Silver Bottle Cap" to maximize one stat of your choice
  • Maximize EV: Use "Feathers" for 1EV each and "Vitamins" for 10EV each
  • Learn recommended attacks: Use "TMs" or level up
  • Check your ability: Does your Pokémon already have its recommended ability? If not, use "Ability Capsule" to switch between both of your abilities or use "Ability Patch" to switch to your hidden ability
  • Check your Tera Type: Does your Pokémon already have the recommended Tera Type? If not: Collet 50 "Tera Shards"
  • Get prefered item: Give your Pokémon your preferred item. If you don't have it, look up where you can find it!

Overview: Dragonite Moveset

In this example, let us train a Dragonite, a solid S-Tier Pokémon. Here we use a physical sweeper build to maximize attack power while being extremely fast in order to assure to attack before your opponent:
  • Level 100
  • Nature: Jolly (+Speed, -SpATK)
  • DV All stats maximized, but maximizing SpATK is not mandatory, just optional
  • EV Spread: 4HP, 252 ATK, 252 Speed
  • Attacks: Outrage, Hurricane, Extreme Speed, Fire Punch
  • Ability: Multiscale
  • Tera Type: Normal
  • Item: Weakness Policy
Outrage is Dragonite strongest move and can outright KO Pokemon that don't resist or are immune to Dragon Type moves. Hurricane is its secondary stab and also quite powerful, although inaccurate. Extreme Speed is a powerful priority move that goes before other priorty attacks, such as Quick Attack. It also gains STAB when Dragonite Terastallizes into a Normal Type, and allows Dragonite to potentially knock out faster but frailer threats. Its final move is Fire Punch, providing great neutral coverage and hits Steel Type threats like Gholdengo and Scizor for super effective damage. This build uses Weakness Policy, an item that raises the Pokemon's attack and special attack by two stages when it is hit by a super-effective move. Thanks to Dragonite's Multiscale Ability, it can survive a super-effective hit and get a boost from its item!

Training

First of all, you have to own a Dragonite. If not, make sure to catch one in a Tera Raid. Alternively, catch a Dratini or Dragonair and evolve it to Dragonite. BE CAREFUL: If you have to train and evolve a Dratini or Dragonair, do not send them to fight! Instead, use Candy to make sure to avoid distributing EV you don't need! When you have a Dragonite, follow these steps:
  1. Train until your Dragonite reaches Level 99! To achieve this, use Candy! You can get Candy easily by doing some high level Tera Raids!
  2. Check the nature of your Dragonite! It needs to have a jolly nature. If it hasn't, use a "Jolly Mint".
  3. Check our DV by using your Pokémon Box! Use "Bottle Caps" by visiting Montenevera and talking to the guy next to the snowman behind the Pokémon Center. As mentioned before, you don't need to maximize your SpATK stat.
  4. Allocate your EV! Use 4 "Health Feathers", 25 "Protein" and 2 "Muscle Feathers", 25 "Carbon" and 2 "Speed Feathers"!
  5. Attacks: Outrage, Fire Punch and Extreme Speed can be learned directly by remembering these attacks. Hurricane can be learnt by using "TM 160".
  6. Ability: Multiscale This is the hidden ability of Dragonite. To learn it, use a "Ability Patch"!
  7. Check your Tera Type: It needs to be a "Normal" Tera Type. If it isn't, collect 50 "Normal Tera Piece" and visit woman sitting next to the bar in the restaurant in Medali to change your current Tera Type to "Normal"!
  8. Give your Dragonite the item "Weakness Policy"! Weakness Policy can be optained in the "Delibird Presents Shop" in Mesagoza for 50000 Pokémon Dollars.
  9. Reach level 100 by using one "Rare Candy"!
